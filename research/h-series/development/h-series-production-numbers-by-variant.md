## Summary

This document compiles all available production numbers, sales data, and registration statistics for Honda H-series engines by variant code and production year. Total confirmed vehicle-level production across the 4th and 5th generation Prelude is 156,745 units (98,627 for 4th gen, 58,118 for 5th gen). Engine-block serial code ranges provide partial production estimates for individual variants. European Accord Type R (CH1) production totaled 3,789 units (UK: 2,081). JDM Accord Euro R (CL1) production was fewer than 2,000 units. USDM 5th gen Prelude clearance sales in 2002-2003 totaled 1,976 units. No official per-engine production data exists from Honda; all figures are derived from vehicle-level production reports, registration databases, and enthusiast community sources.

## Notes

- **Cross-checking:** The 98,627 / 58,118 figures are consistently reported across Wikipedia, Facebook enthusiast groups, and Hagerty Media. These are considered reliable.
- **Conflict — CL1 Euro R count:** Honda-Tech states "<2,000 units ever made" while Honda News says "~3,400 Accord Type Rs were sold in Europe and Japan" combined. If CH1 = ~2,000 (per Wikipedia), then CL1 = ~1,400. The "<2,000" figure from Honda-Tech is conservative and likely accurate.
- **Serial code interpretation:** Engine block serial ranges indicate starting block numbers for each variant/application, not cumulative production. They should be used for identification, not precise production counting.
- **Survival rates:** The ~6.6% survival rate for Accord Type R in the UK is notably low, reflecting aggressive usage. Preludes likely have similar or worse survival rates given their track day and modification culture.
- **No official Honda data:** Every figure in this document is derived from secondary sources. Honda Motor Co. does not publish per-engine production statistics.
